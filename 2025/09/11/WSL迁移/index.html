WSL迁移Linux

导出

关闭wsl

  • wsl -l –all -v 查看当前ubuntu
  • wsl –shutdown

导出wsl包

  • wsl –export Ubuntu D:\soft_pack\wsl_tar\ubuntu_2004_24031501.tar

导入

关闭WSL

  • wsl –shutdown

清除现有WSL

  • wsl –unregister Ubuntu-20.04

导入WSL

  • wsl –import Ubuntu-20.04 D:\sofware\obj\wsl\Ubuntu-20.04 - D:\sofware\obj\ubuntu\ubuntu_2004_24031501.tar –version 2

修改配置/etc/wsl.conf

[user]
default=snow
[interop]
enabled = false
appendWindowsPath = false
